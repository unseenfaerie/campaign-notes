Anash

A midsized agrarian city on the border between the Weinmere and Wavethorn. A busy road bisects it.

Home town of Polly. Rel has visited Anash as well.

Government

Mayor - Galdore

The town is run by the mayor, Galdore. Galdore is a peace-loving man, born and raised in Anash. He is very wise and serves as a sage, one of the only known in Othlorin. His husband perished over twenty years ago in service as a soldier to Count Jirakby's father. Galdore generally makes decisions single-handedly in town, sometimes calling upon trusted members of the community (unless orders are handed down by Count Jirakby). The townspeople have great faith in Galdore as he is gifted in divination and is notoriously even-tempered.

Count - Jirakby

Galdore answers to Count Jirakby, as Anash is within the Weinmere. Jirakby demands taxes in exchange for protection. In times of turmoil, he also demands Galdore's sagely abilities. Jirakby's men have a small barracks in town and serve as its guards. The count is generally hated by the townsfolk of Anash. He rules strictly and enacts laws that are out of touch with the values of the commoners. Some guards are liked as individuals, but by and large they are considered enemies unless proven otherwise. As a side note, the townspeople hate Count Jerushulo even more than they hate Jirakby. He's seen as a blubbering idiot and a needlessly brutish individual. They blame him for the current state of warmongering.

King - Gytha Ellyn IV

King Gytha has greatly surpassed her father's rule, uniting the shambles of the poor counties into a fortified kingdom. This isn't to say that things are going perfectly; her meteoric rise has incited fervor in the Counts, each striving to demonstrate dominance in hopes for favor from the crown. Because of this, the people of Anash begrudgingly honor her rule. Additionally, she is the current matriarch of the Ordoss bloodline (more on the Ordoss in the history section), making her a blood enemy of some townsfolk that carry Khet lineage.

Establishments

The Shimmering Fox - Inn

The town's main inn, right in the center of town. Run by Aberdeen and Astoria Palleday and their daughter Minnie. A temporary home to merchants, priests, messengers, and the occasional adventurer. They provide middle-of-the-road accommodations - not luxurious, but certainly not terrible.

Tersi's Hammer - Blacksmith

Run by Tersi and her wife Agatha. Tersi is the forgemaster, while Agatha runs the business end of things. They largely make a living arming the local guard, merchant cart protectors, and Jirakby's army. Tersi is also an amateur storyteller, and has written 3 of the 24 books that can be borrowed from Supplies and More.

By the Board - Carpenter

Run by Martin Dobeth. He is married to Joba, the Wheelwright. They have four children.
