[Wavethorn]

A merchant's city that is a major hub from Othlorin to the rest of the world. Head city to many local villages.

Arrivalplace of [Releas Scarnewman], [Alann Barnett], [Durchir], and [Cormac].

Government

The Three Councils

Three Councils (Judicial, Mercantile, and ) rule over [Wavethorn], with many members on each council. [Bert] Verinwort serves as an interpreter of the laws that make the framework of the government, as he created them. This system was put in place as a result of the [Wavethorn] Coup of 200 (OOTID - Sessions 1 and 2). Notably, [Gereg] sits on the Mercantile Council since he was able to help supply the [Wavethorn] city guard for the revolution.

Religions

Ponat, God of the Fortress

The city holds the Stalwart Thorn of the Sea, the fortified lighthouse temple and home to the worshippers of Ponat. Most of the townspeople embrace Ponat's presence in their town, especially the Achiel worshippers who respect Ponat for building the Fortress of the Gods where Achiel himself resides. The worshippers themselves protect [Wavethorn] faithfully and without significant or regular pay from the government.

History

Town Founded - Age of the Ascension

Founded by the Rohei family in association with the Ordoss family following the extinction of the Orc Scourge. Heinrich Rohei was able to secure ownership of [Wavethorn] as a result of his aid against Helia Khet in the Battle of Khondorul Fortress.

Coup of [Wavethorn] - May 200 - Sessions 1 and 2

A newly formed, and frankly ragtag, group of adventurers overthrow the government through investigation of the corrupt members of the former council. Aided by finding the coffers of money embezzled by Councilor Daniel Hillstop and Councilor Brae Novan, the adventurers turn the money into a cash bribe to the city guard to remove the old council and enfore the will of the new Three Councils.
