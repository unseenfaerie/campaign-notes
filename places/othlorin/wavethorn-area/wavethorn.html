Wavethorn

A merchant's city that is a major hub from Othlorin to the rest of the world. Head city to many local villages.

Arrivalplace of [Releas Scarnewman], [Alann Barnett], [Durchir], and [Cormac].

Government

The Three Councils

Three Councils (Judicial, Mercantile, and ) rule over Wavethorn, with many members on each council. [Bert] Verinwort serves as an interpreter of the laws that make the framework of the government, as he created them. This system was put in place as a result of the [Coup of 200]. Notably, [Gereg] sits on the Mercantile Council since he was able to help supply the Wavethorn city guard for the revolution.

Establishments

The Lucky badger is a staple to the workers of the Docks Disctrict and those that work the Merchant's alike. Local bard Bard Name is known to frequent the Tavern and sing about current events.

Religions

[Ponat, God of the Fortress]

The city holds the Stalwart Thorn of the Sea, the fortified lighthouse temple and home to the worshippers of [Ponat]. Most of the townspeople embrace [Ponat]'s presence in their town, especially the [Achiel] worshippers who respect [Ponat] for building the Fortress of the Gods where [Achiel] himself resides. The worshippers themselves protect Wavethorn faithfully and without significant or regular pay from the government.

Notable Figures

Bert Verinwort

A key figure in the [Coup of Wavethorn], Bert served as the interpreter of the laws and helped to establish the new government.

Gereg

An underground leader of the Docks district. Gereg was brought to legitimacy during the Coup of Wavethorn, supplying the money that the revolution was short in exchange for a seat on the Mercantile Council.

Leo

The curator of the Wavethorn Museum and Library. A friend to all those who read. [Rel] has made quite an impression on [Leo].

History

Town Founded - Age of the Ascension

Founded by the Rohei family in association with the [Ordoss] family following the extinction of the Orc Scourge. Heinrich Rohei was able to secure ownership of Wavethorn as a result of his aid against Helia Khet in the Battle of Khondorul Fortress.

[The Coup of Wavethorn] - May 200 - OOTID Sessions 1 and 2

A newly formed, and frankly ragtag, group of adventurers ([Rel], [Alann], [Cormac], [Durchir]) overthrow the government through investigation of the secret Wyvernfang members of the former council. After finding the coffers of money embezzled by Councilor Daniel Hillstop and Councilor Brae Novan, the adventurers turn the money into a cash bribe to the city guard to remove the old council and enforce the will of the new Three Councils.
