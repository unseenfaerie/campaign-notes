DM Summary

Approximately 200 years after the fall of Vokdjinn...

A new adventuring party takes shape. [Rel], [Durchir], [Alann], and [Cormac] come to Othlorin from Gatûn. Some seek the riches that lie in the ruins of the old elven homeland. They settle into [Wavethorn], a merchant's city on the edge of the sea. Before long, they are suspected of murder. Their confidant [Bert] Verinwort is later framed for a demonic ritual murder of several prominent figures in town, including his uncle Phil Verinwort. After being kidnapped by Wyvernfang bandits, the party understands the conspiracy to remove political threats to those that the Wyvernfang have installed on the council. Brae Novan and Daniel Hillstop are connected to the gang. After gathering evidence against these parties, the party clears a nearby dungeon of Wyvernfang and uses a massive amount of money they found to bankroll a coup of the government. The coup succeeds and a new, more balanced, three-council-oligarchy is implemented by [Bert]. This endeavor was made possible by an underground crime lord named [Gereg]. Due to his involvement, the party was obliged to put him into power on the Mercantile council. Brae and Daniel escaped execution by fleeing the town before the new regime was enacted.

[Rel]'s Notes

Day 1: The 9 day voyage to [Wavethorn] is complete! It is a trade hub with 6 monarch-type elders. [Ponat] is the Fortress God whose followers inhabit the lighthouse. Wyvernfang are bandits held off by the [Ponat] worshippers.

Districts of [Wavethorn]: Docks, Market, Residential, Commerce, Outskirts

[Idona] is the patron god of Humanity.

I met 3 well-meaning adventurers by the names of [Durchir] (a kindly, serious man), [Cormac] (a soft-spoken and soft-hearted young man), and Barnett (a very serious, but good person). We discovered that there was a string of disappearances in town, which we plan on investigating tomorrow. Our guide, Invae, is coming too.

Day 2: Invae is gone! We were alerted by guards and brought before the council, but quickly acquitted. On our way there, we lost [Cormac]. After visiting the market district briefly, we were at an impasse. [Cormac] was taken into custody for evading arrest earlier in the day. This is pretty saddening.

We, after a few errands, found Verinwort and went to the bar with him. He told us of a time he was kidnapped and taken to some ruins. When we took him home, 2 thieves jumped out the windows. We stopped them and subdued one. We found a demonic altar in his house and several corpses. We also found a notebook detailing the murders of Verinwort's uncle, Invae, and Daré who is scheduled to die in 2 days. Daré is the elder councilwoman so we waited there.

Day 3: In the morning, we discovered Verinwort was dead. After pleading his case to the council, I and the others (aside from [Durchir]) are arrested. In jail, a shadowy figure breaks us out but then knocks us all unconscious. We awake in a different prison, which we break out of instantly.

I come to, being smuggled back to the city, now a wanted man. How did it come to this? Who is orchestrating this? I'm going to try to appeal to the [Ponat] worshippers while [Cormac] recovers our items. However, it is too late and I have to rest. Never being a dull moment, [Bert] was delivered to me, drunk and alive! He woke up the next day and directed me to [Gereg], an underground leader of sorts. After some uneventful deliberation about council members, [Gereg] told me that the ruins I was unconscious in were actually a mausoleum accessible only by the founding family of this city, the Ordoss family. I paid him off through promise to rescue [Cormac] and [Alann] from prison. I truly hope they are safe. We know someone is trying to topple this city and I need to know who. I will not stand for the murder of innocents, no matter what. I may be weak, but I can use what power I have for the greater good.

Council members:

  • Daré - Hella old lady. Must save!
  • [Bert] - Well-meaning youngster. Ally!
  • Wright Belvie - Smuggles in poison and drugs from the elven tropics.
  • Daniel Hillstop - Greedy old bastard, suspected of embezzling.
  • Devon Welshire - Raised by barbarians, later became a scholar.
  • Brae - Husband killed mistakenly by [Ponat] worshippers in Wyvernfang raid.

We now scheme on how to get the key from the Wyvernfang. Upon arriving at the mausoleum AFTER recovering our gear via [Alann], we encounter the same shadowy figure who captured us earlier. Upon losing him, we obtained access to the mausoleum through some regrettable methods... as long as it serves the greater good. While my comrades fought off bandits, I kept [Bert] safe. We entered the secret library type passage in hopes of saving Invae, although I fear it may be too late. I damn my weakness. I truly wish I could be something more than the voice of reason in this party. Alas, we did find her. I left her and [Bert] together for the time being while my allies and I clear out this dungeon. With little success, we escaped after a small run-in with ghost-types.

The next day, we meet with [Gereg] again, this time showing him the note we found in the ruins. He confirms that this is Daniel Hillstop's handwriting, and so we now have solid proof of this. After we recovered an absurd amount of gold from the dungeon, we paid off the captain of the guard to stage a revolution. We broke even, and now plan to hold those responsible accountable. We have successfully instituted a new government for the people. I am pleased.

I want to find new spells. I'm searching the library, looking for any magic users, or nearby ones/sources of magic. I spend my time studying this and such.

I now know that everyone powerful/magical is heading east.
