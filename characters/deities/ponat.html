[Ponat]

[Ponat] is the god of Fortress. He is known for creating the impenetrable walls of the Keep of [Achiel], where the gods reside.

Pantheon

Main Human Pantheon

Alignment and Ethos

[Ponat] is Lawful Good. He embodies the feeling of safety that comes from knowing one's place in the grander workings of life. Each of his faithful link together to form a bullwark around one another and those fortunate enough to be proximate.

Spheres Granted

Protection

Associations

  • [Achiel, God of Light] - Liege Lord

    • Gelmira, Hunter for the Sunfold - Ally

Main Sects

Worshippers of [Ponat]

Based in the Lighthouse of [Wavethorn], the worshippers of [Ponat] are known for their dedication to physical protection. They believe in the strength of walls and the safety of their community, there they find [Ponat]'s love in the form of security. Their main form of worship is dutiful guardianship of the city of [Wavethorn] and the maintenance of their walls and fortifications.
