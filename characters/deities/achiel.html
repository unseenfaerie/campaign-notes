[Achiel]

The patron god of the sun. His light and love give order to the world. Those that worship [Achiel] are vast within the Othlorin. There are many sect of his worship, ranging from the Templars of [Achiel] to the moral justiciars of The Church of [Achiel]'s Light.

Pantheon

Main

Alignment and Ethos

The sun god is Lawful Good. He represents the concepts of goodness, life, forgiveness, and consistency. He is generosity, though Rameen, the god of the elves sometimes decides to hide [Achiel]'s wisdom from the skies with clouds and rain.

Sun, Healing, Protection, Creation

Main Sects

The Church of [Achiel]'s Light

A rigorous and outwardly severe religious oligarchy that has great influence in the city of Novafell. This form of worship considers penance the most important form of love for [Achiel]. They offer everything and live modestly by a strict moral code. Punishments are as important as the rules they compliment. Should you die while in a favorable countenance with [Achiel], you shall live an eaon of days as a welcome guest in his wonderous realm of rolling, sun soaked wheat fields.
