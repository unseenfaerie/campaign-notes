Wyaris

Overview

Pantheon

Three Sister Goddesses

Alignment and Ethos

Wyaris is Chaotic Good. She represents the reckless selflessness that people can undertake. Of the Three Sisters, Wyaris is the most tenacious. She embodies the ugency of "NOW!".

Spheres Granted

War,

Associations

  • [Danaris, Lady of Death] - Sister
  • [Vaharis, Lady of Judgement] - Sister

Main Sects

The [Winged Blades of Wyaris]

Die-hard zealots of the Dancing Blade form passed down by Wyaris. Their edict is to go out amongst the people and ensure that good is done, laws be damned. Most common people are heartened to see them, especially on the road where their presence reduces ambushes by [Wyvernfang].
