[Idona]

The patron goddess of and mother to Humankind. Her nurturing guidance shows us what we need to know to thrive. Those that worship [Idona] are numerous within the Othlorin. She is primarily worshipped as [Achiel]'s Wife, deserving of respect and credence. There are some women who have dedicated their lives to interpreting the messages of the moon as those are what [Idona] intends.

Pantheon

Main

Alignment and Ethos

The goddess of Humankind is Chaotic Good. She represents the concepts of love between all beings, cycles of life, intuition, and devotion. She, like [Achiel], is sometimes blocked and obscured by the efforts of Rameen.

Spheres Granted

Moon, Healing, Charm, Protection

Associations

[Achiel, God of Light] - Husband

Ephas, Prince of Virtue - Son

Myri, Princess of Angels - Daughter

Gelmira, Hunter for the Sunfold - Demigod

Chasya, The Misting Sea - Demigod

Ponat, God of Fortress - Demigod

Main Sects

The Church of [Achiel]'s Light

[Idona]'s place in the church is the role of the Mother Saint. She protects us all in [Achiel]'s name. She raises and teaches her other children: Ephas and Myri, the Empyrians.

The Daughters of [Idona]

A secretive sect of clergywomen. They favor night time and study the moon above, searching for meaning and guidance.
