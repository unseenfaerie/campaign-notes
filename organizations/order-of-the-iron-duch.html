The Order of the Iron Düch

Basic Info

An adventuring party consisting of [Releas Scarnewman], [Alann Barnett], [Cormac], and Constellis Tyrannus. Named for the fallen [Durchir] after The [Night of Spiders].

Areas of Operation

[Wavethorn] - Adventuring Party

The Order of the Iron Düch has been a stalwart presence in the city of [Wavethorn]. Though they often get in over their heads, they make sure to clean up the messes they make and do the right thing for the people of [Wavethorn].
