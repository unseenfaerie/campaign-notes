The Adventurer's Guild

Basic Info

A guild formed by famous heroes in Novafell. This guild aims to organize those with a knack for mercenary work, dungeon delving, monster slaying, and other dirty tasks. Members are paid for work that they do and gain ranks within the guild as they complete work, eventually taking on adventuring parties as teachers and leaders.

Areas of Operation

Novafell - Guild

The Adventurer's Guild in Novafell is the main headquarters of the organization. Their leader, Aereth Dragonslayer resides and mentors there. The guild is part of the new money Guilds in the city.
