Wyvernfang

Basic Info

A syndicate of bandits that plague the [Wavethorn] region. They prey on traders leaving the city who are laden with goods from the world at large.

Areas of Operation

[Wavethorn] - Bandits

Engaging in ambushes and raids on merchant caravans, the Wyvernfang are known for their guerilla tactics. Their leadership has also historically installed agents within [Wavethorn]'s government system. Wyvernfang bandits operate in small units, usually hiding out in the woods when not at their main base.
